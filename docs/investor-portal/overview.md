# EaaSGrid Investor Portal Overview

## Purpose

The EaaSGrid Investor Portal is the public-facing presentation platform
for EaaSGrid Platform Ltd.

It communicates the Energy-as-a-Service infrastructure model,
investment opportunity, deployment strategy and partnership
opportunities.

---

## Objectives

The portal provides:

- Company positioning
- Energy infrastructure challenge explanation
- EaaSGrid solution presentation
- Business model explanation
- Market opportunity
- Financial opportunity overview
- Investor and partner engagement

## Main Sections

The portal contains:

### Hero

Introduces EaaSGrid and the Energy-as-a-Service vision.

### Problem

Explains the African energy infrastructure challenge.

### Solution

Describes the EaaSGrid managed energy infrastructure model.

### Business Model

Explains recurring energy infrastructure revenue.

### Market

Describes target sectors and expansion opportunity.

### Financials

Presents pilot investment requirements and scaling opportunity.

### Roadmap

Shows deployment phases.

### Contact

Provides investor and partnership engagement information.
